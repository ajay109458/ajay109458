## About Me

I’m Ajay Kumar Yadav, a Staff Software Engineer at Google with a background across Microsoft and Disney+ Hotstar, focused on large-scale cloud infrastructure, platform engineering, and systems design.

My work has centered on building and operating infrastructure where failure is expensive and scale is non-negotiable: sovereign cloud environments, Kubernetes orchestration, DNS at massive throughput, and developer/platform systems that need to be secure, observable, and boring in the best possible way.

What I care about most:
- Building software that is reliable before it is impressive
- Designing systems that scale without becoming fragile
- Keeping the engineering bar high while reducing operational toil
- Making complexity understandable, measurable, and automatable

I like to think in terms of leverage: if a change improves reliability, developer velocity, and operational clarity at the same time, it is probably the right change.
